# Graphics_Game_Project
Final Project for Computer Graphics Course, AUEB 2026

Goal was to create a navigable 3d environment with complex lighting and shadows, with a time based goal.

Game goal is to navigate to and mine asteroids with ores before the limited and depleting energy of the craft runs out. 

## Controls:

SPACE: Mine with laser

W/S: Nose Up and Down

Q/E: Roll Left and Right

A/D: Yaw Left and Right

Up / Down Arrows: Accelerate Forwards and Backwards

Left / Right Arrows: Accelerate Left and Right

Right Shift / Control: Accelerate Upwards and Downwards

Left Control: Decelerate
